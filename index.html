Who are we?

Board game club is a student-run organization that consists of friends who enjoy bonding over our mutual love of tabletop and card games. We are inclusive, supportive, and welcoming to players of any experience level, and our main mission is to make sure that everyone has fun and finds their home with us! So if you're interested on learning more about us and the type of events we host, feel free to scroll around on this page and see what we have to offer!

Where do we meet?

We meet every Thursday from 7-9pm in ENGEO! Please stop by and say hi! We love meeting new people and are always accepting new members who are looking for board-game loving friends!

Member dues? For what?

In board game club we have dues that are $15. This goes towards many things including club copies of board games, special events, and discounts on t-shirts! It really does help the club afloat. However, you do not need to pay dues to come to meetings and play games with us!
